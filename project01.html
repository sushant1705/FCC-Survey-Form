SUSHANT SINGH RAJPUT

♥♥The man with a Billon Hearts♥♥

Here is how the acting career of SSR looks like:

  • 2008 - The Balaji Telefilms casting team saw one of the stage plays of Sushant Singh Rajput for Ekjute and was invited for auditions. He was offered the role of Preet Juneja in 'Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil'. He became a popular face in every Indian household after this role.
  • 2009 - He starred in the daily soap 'Pavitra Rishta' and played the role of Manav Deshmukh.
  • 2010 - He participated in the dance reality show 'Zara Nachke Dikha 2' and became part of the Mast Kalandar Boys Team.
  • 2011 - He quit Pavitra Rishta after two years to pursue a film-making course abroad.
  • 2013 - Sushant Singh Rajput made his film debut with 'Kai Po Che!' and played the role of one of the leads in the film alongside Rajkumar Rao and Amit Sadh. The same year, he was offered his second Bollywood movie 'Shuddh Desi Romance' alongside Parineeti Chopra and Vaani Kapoor.
  • 2014 - He played a minor role in 'PK' starring Aamir Khan and Anushka Sharma. The film is one of the highest-grossing Indian films.
  • 2015 - He starred in a lead role in a mystery thriller 'Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!'.
  • 2016 - He appeared in 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' in the lead role. The film became one of the highest-grossing films of Bollywood that year.
  • 2017 - He co-starred with Kriti Sanon in 'Raabta'.
  • 2018 - He was seen in 'Kedarnath' alongside Sara Ali Khan.
  • 2019 - He appeared in two films-- Sonchiriya (opposite Bhumi Pednekar) and Chhichhore (opposite Shraddha Kapoor).
